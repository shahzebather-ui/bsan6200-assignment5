Data Analyst - Coframe

About the job


What You'll Do:

Respond directly to customer data and analytics requests
Build ad-hoc reports and answer business questions quickly
Align metrics across multiple analytics systems
Investigate and resolve data discrepancies
Build tools and automate recurring requests
Help turn one-off requests into internal products and processes


You May Be a Fit If:

Strong skills building queries, reports, and dashboards in complex data environments
Clear communication with non-technical teams internally and on the client side
Clear communication with data and technical teams on both sides
Ability to move fast while staying careful and accurate in conclusions
Strong adoption of AI tools, with good judgment about their strengths and limits
Good prioritization skills across many incoming requests


Bonus Points:

Experience with Optimizely, VWO, LaunchDarkly, or similar experimentation platforms.
Experienced with GA4, Segment, Amplitude, or Heap and understand how data flows from browser event to dashboard.
Familiarity with Google Tag Manager or Tealium.
Exposure to Shopify or SaaS product environments.
You manage competing priorities across multiple client engagements without losing quality.
Excited about AI-powered experimentation and autonomous agents.


Compensation 

Base: $100,000 - $170,000
Equity: Meaningful early-stage equity.


Final offer amounts are determined by multiple factors including experience and expertise.

Business Intelligence Analyst - Insurance Risk Analytics

About the job

Position Overview


The Business Analyst/ Business Intelligence Analyst supports the business units by delivering reporting, system configuration, and analytical solutions that drive operational efficiency and informed decision-making. This role works closely with internal stakeholders to gather business requirements, translate needs into technical solutions, and support both existing and new insurance programs.


Job Description:

Support project management activities for internal systems and process improvements
Assist with data migration and data validation efforts
Gather, document, and refine business requirements from underwriting and operational teams
Support management of software development efforts with third-party vendors
Contribute to project planning, task tracking, and milestone coordination
Participate in RFP processes for new software solutions as needed
Perform underwriting, operational, and financial analysis using internal and external data sources
Develop and maintain SQL queries for reporting, data exports, and ad hoc analysis
Build intuitive reports and visualizations for business users and leadership
Configure and administer systems for existing and new insurance programs
Support troubleshooting, testing, and user acceptance testing (UAT) activities
Create and maintain Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) and Technical Specifications to ensure clarity, repeatability, and proper system documentation
Additional responsibilities may be assigned as directed by management.


Required Job Qualifications:

1–2+ years of experience in a Business Analyst, Data Analyst, or similar analytical role
Working knowledge of SQL for querying and reporting – 1+ year
Experience working with databases, Microsoft Excel, and reporting tools
Ability to gather and clearly document business requirements
Strong verbal and written communication skills
Strong organizational and time-management skills


Preferred Job Qualifications:

Background in Finance, Mathematics, Computer Science, or a related field
Experience in the insurance industry
Exposure to Salesforce or Insurance Management Systems (IMS)
Exposure to Microsoft Azure ecosystem (e.g., Azure Logic Apps, Azure Data Factory, Power Apps)
Experience with data visualization tools
Experience with Visual Basic for Applications

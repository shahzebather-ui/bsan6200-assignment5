Investment Operations Automation Analyst - Tamar Securities


About the job

Tamar Securities is seeking an Investment Operations Automation Analyst to design, build, and maintain automated workflows supporting trading and investment operations as the firm scales.


This role sits at the intersection of trading, operations, and technology. The ideal candidate has a strong operational mindset, understands the importance of data integrity in trade execution, and has the technical skill set to replace manual, spreadsheet-driven processes with automated, scalable systems.


As account volume and customization increase, this role will be critical in ensuring trade implementation remains efficient, accurate, and controlled.


Key Responsibilities

Trading & Investment Operations Automation
Design and build automated pre-trade workflows to validate account data, trade eligibility, and portfolio customization
Develop tools that streamline trade readiness across multiple accounts and strategies
Automate model-to-account mapping, drift analysis, and exception reporting
Reduce manual review and spreadsheet dependency in trade implementation processes


Data Pipelines & Systems Integration

Build and maintain automated data pipelines between custodians, portfolio management systems (e.g., Tamarac), and internal tools
Ensure data accuracy, consistency, and auditability across systems
Create standardized datasets and reports used by trading and investment teams
Identify, troubleshoot, and permanently resolve data breaks or workflow failures


Workflow & Process Design

Analyze existing operational workflows and identify opportunities for automation
Design scalable, repeatable processes that support account growth and customization
Implement controls, checks, and logging to mitigate operational and trading risk
Document workflows, systems, and logic clearly for internal use


Cross-Functional Collaboration

Work closely with portfolio management, trading, operations, and compliance teams
Translate investment and operational requirements into technical solutions
Support ongoing enhancements as strategies, constraints, and account complexity develop


Required Qualifications

Advanced proficiency in Excel (complex formulas, Power Query, structured models)
Strong working knowledge of Python for data processing, automation, and workflow logic
Experience working with large, multi-account datasets
Strong attention to detail and understanding of operational risk in trading environments
Ability to design, build, test, and maintain automation solutions independently
Certified CFA level 3 - Pass the three levels of the CFA exams


Preferred Qualifications

Experience in an RIA, asset manager, hedge fund, custodian, or trading operations role
Familiarity with portfolio management platforms such as Tamarac or similar systems
Experience with APIs, SQL, or cloud-based data tools
Exposure to trading workflows or customized portfolio implementations
Background in finance, economics, computer science, or a related field


Computer systems required:
Tamarac, Redtail, Excel Professional, Computer Science technician, preferably registered with a Series 65, RIA wealth management firm.

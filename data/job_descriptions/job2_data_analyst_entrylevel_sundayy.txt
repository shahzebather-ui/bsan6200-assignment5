Data Analyst, Entry-Level - Sundayy


About The Role

The Entry-Level Data Analyst position offers an exciting opportunity for recent graduates or individuals looking to launch their career in data analysis. This remote role involves collecting, organizing, and analyzing data from various sources to uncover valuable insights that can inform strategic decisions. You will work closely with project managers and other team members to identify key performance metrics, develop reports and dashboards, and present your findings in a clear and actionable manner. This role provides comprehensive training and mentorship to help you develop essential skills in data analysis, reporting, and business intelligence. It is an ideal position for a detail-oriented individual eager to grow professionally within a supportive environment.

Qualifications

Bachelor's degree in mathematics, statistics, computer science, or a related field
Strong analytical and critical thinking skills
Proficiency in data analysis tools such as Microsoft Excel, SQL, or Tableau
Excellent communication and presentation abilities
Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team
Detail-oriented with strong organizational skills
Ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously and meet deadlines

Responsibilities

Collect and organize data from various internal and external sources
Analyze datasets to identify trends, patterns, and anomalies
Create comprehensive reports and interactive dashboards to visualize data insights
Collaborate with team members to define key metrics and identify areas for operational improvement
Use data insights to recommend process enhancements and cost-saving measures
Monitor data accuracy and relevance through continuous evaluation and validation
Communicate findings effectively to team members and management through presentations and reports
Support ongoing data collection and analysis initiatives to foster data-driven decision making

Benefits

Competitive salary package
Comprehensive training program to develop your skills
Flexible work schedule allowing for work-life balance
Opportunities for career growth and professional development within the company
Work from the comfort of your own home, supporting remote work flexibility
